# IBM HR Attrition Analysis

## Business Problem
IBM wants to understand why employees are leaving the company. This analysis identifies the key factors driving attrition and provides actionable recommendations to help HR reduce turnover.

## Dataset
- Source: IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition & Performance (Kaggle)
- 1,470 employees | 35 features
- Target variable: Attrition (Yes/No)

## Tools
Python | pandas | matplotlib | seaborn

## Key Findings
- **Overall Attrition Rate:** 16.12%
- **Highest Attrition Department:** Sales (20.63%)
- **Highest Attrition Age Group:** 18-25 (34.78%)
- **Highest Attrition Job Role:** Sales Representative (39.76%)
- **Income Gap:** Employees who left earned $2,045 less on average
- **Overtime:** Employees working overtime leave at 3x the rate (30.53% vs 10.44%)

## Business Recommendations
1. Review compensation for junior and mid-level roles
2. Audit overtime policies — strongest attrition driver
3. Focus retention efforts on Sales department
4. Build early career programs for employees aged 18-25
5. Prioritize Sales Representatives for targeted retention incentives
